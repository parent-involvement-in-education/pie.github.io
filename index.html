Mission

We encourage parents to participate and take initiatives in children’s education.



Learn More

Early childhood education is fundamental to future success; however, parental involvement in education still remains a quality of life issue as 66% of the respondents claimed that they never or rarely participate in their children’s school activities. To solve the issue, we coded a website to spread awareness about the issue, created a raffle prize system, cooperated with the school to introduce the CareMonkey app, and plan to encourage a government tax credit base on parent involvement.

Quality of Life

Education is a component of the quality of life index. The better the educaion, the higher the quality of life score.

The Power of Parents

Parents can provide motivation, offer guidance, and support students socially and academically.

Road to Success

Research has show that with parental support, students tend to gain better test scores.
