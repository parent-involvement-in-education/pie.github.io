About Us

This is a Quality of Life project conducted by Xiurong Yu and Wei Zheng. With the interest in education and first-generation college student background, we understand the difficulty involving the lack of parental involvement in academics. With the decreasing trend of parent engagement we notice in our school, we decided to create a project that would emphasize the importance of parents’ role in education. We hope that this project will spread awareness on the issue of parental involvement in education and be a resource for parents to reference back.


If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, feel free to complete our survey below and give us feedback.
